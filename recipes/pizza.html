Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza consists of a flattened disk of bread dough topped
with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato,
pepperoni, mozzarella or other cheeses, and many other
ingredients baked quickly at high temperatures.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces of pizza dough this can be store bought or homemade(this makes enough dough for 2 pizzas)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (store bought or homemade)
  • 18 to 20 pieces of pepperoni
  • 12 ounces of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano(optional)
  • flour for rolling and shaping dough

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 500 degrees.

  2. Prep your sauce if you're making your own mix ingredients or have store bought sauce ready.

  3. Set your dough out til its room temperature and roll your dough on a flat surface.

  4. Place dough into large cast iron add your sauce into a light layer all over the pizza leaving 1/4 inch crust around edges. Top the pizza with grated mozzarella cheese, Pepperoni slices, and season with black pepper.


  5. Place skillet over a high heat burner for 2 minutes to get it preheated and get the crust cooking right away. Then transfer to the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes or until crust is golden brown.

  6. Let the pizza cool and slice into desired amount of slices and enjoy!
